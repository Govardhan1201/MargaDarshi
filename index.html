Know Your Bus

City Ordinary vs
Metro Express

Vizag has two types of APSRTC city buses — choose the right one for your journey.

🚌

City Ordinary

Base fare from ₹7 · Stops at all designated stops
CO
  • ✅ Cheapest option — fare starts at ₹7
  • ✅ Operates on all major routes across the city
  • ✅ Stops at every designated bus stop on the route
  • ✅ Accepts One Day Pass (₹50 unlimited)
  • ✅ Student & senior concessions available
  • ⚠️ Slower — more stops, can get crowded in peak hours
  • ⚠️ Frequency varies — usually every 15–25 minutes
🚍

Metro Express

Fare 1.1x–1.2x of City Ordinary · Limited stops
ME
  • ✅ Faster — serves key stops only, skipping smaller ones
  • ✅ Less crowded — better comfort, especially during peak hours
  • ✅ Operates on select high-demand routes (e.g., Beach Road, IT Corridor)
  • ✅ Air-conditioned variants available on some routes
  • ⚠️ Slightly higher fare (about 10–20% more than City Ordinary)
  • ⚠️ Limited routes — not every corridor has a Metro Express
  • ⚠️ Lower frequency — usually every 30–45 minutes
🎫

APSRTC One Day Pass — ₹100

Unlimited travel on all City Ordinary buses for a full day. If you plan to travel more than ₹100 worth of travel in a day, the Day Pass saves you money. Our Route Finder automatically suggests this when your trip fares exceed ₹100!

Smart Tips

Travel Smarter,
Spend Less

Insider tips to save time, money, and avoid the rush on Vizag city buses.

💰

Save on Fare

If you're making multiple trips in a day totalling over ₹100, buy the APSRTC One Day Pass for unlimited rides. Seniors citizens get 50% off with valid ID.

⏰

Beat the Rush

Peak hours are 7–9 AM and 5–7 PM. Try to board a stop earlier than usual so you're guaranteed a seat before buses fill up.

🚍

Choose Metro Express

For important journeys, pay the small premium for Metro Express — you'll arrive 20–30% faster and with far less crowding.

📍

Board at Jagadamba

Jagadamba Centre is Vizag's main bus hub — virtually every route passes through it. If confused, go there and find your bus with ease.

🔄

Plan Return Journey

Check the last bus timing on your route before you travel — especially for routes to Bheemunipatnam and Anakapalli where last buses run early.

📲

Carry Exact Change

Bus conductors often run short on change. Keep small denomination coins and notes handy for smoother boarding — especially for fares under ₹20.

FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

The APSRTC One Day Pass costs ₹50 and allows unlimited travel on all City Ordinary buses across Visakhapatnam for a single day. You can buy it directly from the conductor when you board your first bus, or at the Jagadamba bus station ticket counter. It's valid only on City Ordinary (CO) buses, not Metro Express.

City Ordinary (CO) buses stop at every designated stop along their route — they're cheaper (fare starts at ₹7) but slower. Metro Express (ME) buses stop only at major stops — they're 10–20% costlier but significantly faster and less crowded. Both are operated by APSRTC. If you're in a hurry, Metro Express is the better choice. For budget travel, City Ordinary wins.

The times shown are the official APSRTC scheduled departure times. However, actual bus arrival times can vary due to traffic congestion, road works, diversions, or mechanical issues. We always show a disclaimer for this reason. For critical journeys, arrive at the stop a few minutes early and rely on community reports for real-time updates.

Students and senior citizens are entitled to a 50% fare concession on all APSRTC city buses. Students must carry a valid school or college ID card. Senior citizens (60+) must carry any valid government-issued age proof such as Aadhar card. Inform the conductor before they issue your ticket.

Login is required to submit community reports to prevent spam and ensure the reliability of information shared with fellow commuters. Simply click "Login to Report" in the Community section and sign in with your email. Once logged in, you can report route diversions, road work, accidents, or any useful bus information for other users.

Peak hours in Visakhapatnam are typically 7:00 AM – 9:00 AM (morning office/school rush) and 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM (evening return rush). During these times, all buses — especially on routes like 28C, 38H, and 400 — will be heavily crowded. If possible, travel before 7 AM or between 10 AM–4 PM for the most comfortable experience.
